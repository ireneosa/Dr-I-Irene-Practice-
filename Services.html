Our Services

Comprehensive medical care for individuals and families — all under one roof in Uitenhage.

What We Offer

Quality Healthcare Services

From routine check-ups to specialised care, our practice provides a broad range of medical services delivered with professionalism and compassion.

🩺
Primary Care

General Consultations

Our practice provides thorough medical consultations for patients of all ages. We take time to listen, assess, and develop personalised treatment plans tailored to your health needs.

  • Physical examinations
  • Acute illness treatment
  • Diagnosis & referrals
  • Prescription management
  • Health screenings
  • Follow-up consultations
❤️
Ongoing Care

Chronic Disease Management

We provide continuous, structured care for patients living with chronic conditions. Our goal is to help you manage your health effectively and maintain the best quality of life.

  • Diabetes management
  • Hypertension control
  • Asthma & COPD care
  • Cholesterol monitoring
  • HIV/AIDS management
  • Regular blood work
💉
Prevention

Vaccinations & Immunisations

Stay protected with routine and travel vaccinations for children and adults. We follow the latest immunisation schedules recommended by the Department of Health.

  • Childhood immunisations
  • Flu vaccinations
  • Travel vaccines
  • COVID-19 boosters
  • Hepatitis B vaccine
  • Tetanus boosters
🩹
In-Office Procedures

Minor Procedures

We perform a range of minor surgical procedures safely in our practice, saving you the inconvenience of a hospital visit for non-complex issues.

  • Wound care & suturing
  • Abscess drainage
  • Ingrown toenail removal
  • Lesion removal
  • Foreign body removal
  • Ear syringing
📋
Documentation

Medical Certificates & Reports

We issue a variety of medical documents required for employment, education, insurance, and legal purposes — all compliant with South African regulations.

  • Sick notes (sick leave)
  • Fitness certificates
  • Medical reports
  • Insurance assessments
  • School health forms
  • Driver's licence medicals
🏢
Workplace Health

Occupational Health Services

We partner with local businesses and employers to ensure their workforce is healthy, fit, and compliant with occupational health regulations.

  • Pre-employment medicals
  • Fitness assessments
  • Periodic health screening
  • Exit medical examinations
  • Drug & alcohol testing
  • Audiometry & spirometry
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Family Care

Family & Women's Health

We provide dedicated healthcare services for the whole family, including women's health screenings, family planning, and child wellness check-ups.

  • Family planning
  • Pap smears
  • Pregnancy tests
  • Child wellness checks
  • Growth monitoring
  • Nutritional guidance
🚨
Urgent Care

After-Hours & Urgent Enquiries

While we are not a 24-hour facility, we accommodate urgent medical concerns during practice hours and can advise you on after-hours options.

  • Same-day appointments
  • Walk-in consultations
  • Urgent care guidance
  • Emergency referrals
  • WhatsApp consultations
  • Telephonic triage
Payment & Medical Aid

Accepted Medical Aids & Payment

We accept most major South African medical aid schemes. Cash and private patients are also welcome. Please bring your medical aid card to every visit.

  • Discovery Health
  • Bonitas
  • GEMS
  • Momentum Health
  • Medshield
  • Bestmed
  • Profmed
  • Other schemes

    • Private & Cash Patients
