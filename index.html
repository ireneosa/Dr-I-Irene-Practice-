About Our Practice

Your Trusted Healthcare Partner in Uitenhage

Dr Ikponmwosa Irene Practice is a trusted healthcare provider located in the heart of Rosedale, Uitenhage. As a HPCSA registered medical practitioner, Dr Irene is committed to delivering professional, patient-centred care to individuals and families.

From routine check-ups to chronic disease management, our practice offers comprehensive medical services in a warm and welcoming environment. We believe in building lasting relationships with our patients based on trust, respect, and quality healthcare delivery.
